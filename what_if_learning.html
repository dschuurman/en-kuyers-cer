"What If Learning" Teacher Resources

Provided by the Kuyers Institute at Calvin University

"What If Learning" arises out of the Charis Project on spiritual and moral development in schools, which was based at the Stapleford Centre in the 1990s and out of more recent work on teaching and Christian practices based at the Kuyers Institute at Calvin University. It has been developed to support schools that aspire to offer a distinctively Christian teaching and learning experience.

These materials are originally taken from http://www.whatiflearning.com. Terms and conditions for use are summarized at the bottom of this page.
